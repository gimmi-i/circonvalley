Circonvalley Bikes

Circonvalley is a cycling community.

Our goal is to help you cultivate your passion for bicycles by connecting you with other enthusiasts. We organize social events and bike rides, we share ideas for new adventures, we help repair and build bikes. We are from Milano, and we know that the bike life can be hard in a big city, and that's why we are here: to support everyone in the discovery of what cycling is all about.

Wether you are a commuter, a bikepacker, a messenger or a bike racer, you are welcome to be part of our community and to contribute to our mission.
